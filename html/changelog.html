21 January 2021

Ghommie updated:

  • jitteriness, melee attack animations and resting/standing up should no longer momentarily disable the floating movetype.
  • Fixed zero damage items with set attack verbs not showing chat messages anyway.

Maurukas updated:

  • PubbyStation subtypes of booze-o-mat and wallmed
  • ERT shuttle access on Kilostation. Interns and other livestock may board again.
  • Misplaced solar wires on Kilo have been corrected
  • Some more minor decals/grille placement on Kilo
  • Spiders will no longer spawn in space on Kilo

Melbert updated:

  • New negative quirk: 'Bad Touch'. With it, you gain a negative moodlet from being hugged instead of a positive one, and you gain a negative moodlet from headpats and grabs.
  • blind people can now feel headpats

Nari Harimoto updated:

  • Kilo Station white ship shuttle dock no longer spawns a redundant mining shuttle

Ryll/Shaps updated:

  • The clown car can once again be driven
  • Mechs can once again open doors by bumping into them (assuming the rider has access, obviously)

Timberpoes updated:

  • You are no longer able to teleport rigged power cells across the station to remotely detonate departments using the BRPED. Rigged cells will instead explode inside the BRPED when you attempt to transfer them. That's right, you can now rig BRPEDs to explode when people use them.

interestingusernam3 updated:

  • Replaced US Indigenous People's Day with it's international equivalent

sergeirocks100 updated:

  • The vacant offices on Ice Box and Delta are now public access.
  • The Ice Box vacant office airlock has been turned into a plain airlock to reflect the fact it no longer needs engineering access to open. It has also been renamed to reflect the fact that Vacant Office B no longer exists.
  • The Meta vacant office airlock has a name now when it was unnamed before.

  • Revheads will no longer fail their over-the-shoulder conversions if they're on help intent.

Ryll/Shaps updated:

  • Dead bodies with a brain but no ghost actively inside the body will once again appear dead when examined

spessbro updated:

zxaber updated:

28 December 2020

Gamer025 updated:

  • Remove broken "name" entries in security techfab under "Ammo" category
  • When being hit by a thrown vendor it crushes you

Seris02 updated:

  • 2 punctuation errors

27 December 2020

Azarak updated:

  • Rotting gibs no longer make miasma on planetary turfs, to save on processing as it quickly dissipiates into planetary atmos anyway. They'll also stop rotting after some time

Ghommie updated:

  • The ashdrake won't become enraged if all lava arena targets have gone clientless or deleted now.

Jared-Fogle updated:

  • Bolas will now make sounds and facehuggers now have a special sprite when thrown, as was intended.

LemonInTheDark updated:

  • Atmos backpacks won't go textureless anymore

Timberpoes updated:

  • Admins may now call the deadchat_plays proc on any movable atom in the game, allowing deadchat to move it around with zero effort. They should now call the generic deadchat_plays instead of deadchat_plays_goose to turn Birdboat into a deadchat controlled vomit goose.
  • The VV dropdown now contains an option to enable or disable deadchat control. This comes with an alert confirmation box to allow you to back out and chat messages to tell you the result.
  • There's now a lot more feedback when deadchat is controlling something, including telling you when command inputs are successful, telling you the remaining cooldown until you can input new commands when it fails and additional examine text detailling the control mode, commands and overall cooldown.

Tlaltecuhtli updated:

  • sausage is crafted with raw meat instead of cooked meat

necromanceranne updated:

  • Uranium golems now no longer irradiate things around them passively.
  • Uranium golems now irradiate targets they punch, while also doing burn damage with punches. They only hit as hard a human, however.
  • Uranium golems are significantly resistant to brute, but less resistant to all other damage types compared to typical golems.
  • Uranium golems release their radiation pulse when hit by attacks. Or objects.
  • Uranium golems can now wear engineering jumpsuits, work boots and wintercoats.
